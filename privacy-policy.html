Magento Extension

Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy describes how this Magento extension handles information. This extension is a software module that runs on your own Magento store. We do not operate a service, collect your data, or have access to your store or its customers.
01.

Overview

This extension is a Magento 2 module distributed through the Adobe Commerce Marketplace. It is installed and operated entirely within your own Magento instance, on servers you control.

The developer of this extension does not collect, store, process, or have access to any data from your store, your administrators, or your customers.

02.

Data We Do Not Collect

This extension does not collect or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal information of store visitors or customers
  • Order, payment, or transaction data
  • Store configuration or credentials
  • Browsing behavior or analytics data
  • Administrator account information
  • Any other data from your Magento store
03.

How the Extension Works

This module operates exclusively within your Magento installation. All processing occurs on your own server infrastructure. No data is sent to external servers operated by the extension developer.

You are the data controller for all information within your Magento store. It is your responsibility to handle customer data in compliance with applicable privacy laws (such as GDPR, CCPA, or KVKK) through your own Privacy Policy.

04.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms by default. If a specific feature of the extension optionally connects to an external service, it will be clearly documented in the extension's README and will require explicit configuration by you.

05.

Cookies & Local Storage

Any cookies or browser storage used by this extension are functional in nature and remain within your store's domain. They are used solely to support the features of the extension and are not used for tracking or advertising purposes.

06.

Log Files & Diagnostics

The extension may write log entries to your Magento log files (e.g., var/log/) for debugging purposes. These logs remain on your server and are not transmitted to the developer.

07.

Your Responsibilities

As the merchant installing this extension, you are responsible for:

  • Maintaining your own Privacy Policy for your store and customers
  • Complying with applicable data protection laws in your jurisdiction
  • Securing your Magento installation and server environment
  • Keeping the extension updated to the latest version
08.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated date at the top of this page and in the repository's commit history. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
